Tile
Description and Scoring
Darkness
T
Tents provide a family with a place to live. Families need food, water, shelter, and light, and they don't want to live next to the Darkness or the edge of the map. Each family allows drawing an additional Tile per round.
  • -2 points touching Darkness or edge of map
  • -1 point for each unfulfilled need (food, water, shelter, light)
W
Wells provide water to nearby families in Tents and Houses. Families don't want to draw water from Wells that are next to the Darkness.
  • -1 point touching Darkness
  • +1 point for every nearby Tent and House
F
Farms provide food for up to 2 families in Tents and Houses. Farms want to be nearby other Farms.
  • +2 points for each Farm in a group
L
Lumber Mills add 2 House tiles to the deck. Families don't want to live next to them.
  • -1 point for each nearby Tent or House
H
Houses are placed on top of Tents and provide a family with shelter.
  • -2 points touching Darkness or edge of map
  • -1 point for each unfulfilled need (food, water, light)
